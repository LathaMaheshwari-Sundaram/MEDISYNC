MEDISYNC is a comprehensive telehealth management platform designed to streamline digital healthcare services and improve patient–doctor interaction. The system consists of five integrated modules. The Patient Profile & Health Records module stores and displays essential patient information, medical history, and allows sharing of medical reports such as lab reports, scan reports, and prescriptions for easy access by doctors during consultations. The Appointment & Scheduling module enables patients to book consultations by selecting hospitals, doctors, dates, and available time slots. The Telehealth Interface supports virtual consultations through secure video links, allowing patients to consult doctors remotely. The Notifications & Messages module provides appointment reminders and medicine refill alerts, helping patients stay updated and maintain treatment adherence. Finally, the Billing Dashboard allows patients to make payments and view their billing history.

Together, these modules create a complete digital healthcare workflow—from patient record management and report sharing to consultation, reminders, and payment processing.
